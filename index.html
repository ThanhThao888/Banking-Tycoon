VOCAB GUIDE
Key Terms
10 Words to Know
Central Bank
= Ngân hàng Trung ương
Controls money supply, lends to other banks in crisis, issues banknotes.
Lender of Last Resort
= Người cho vay cuối cùng
The Central Bank's emergency role — rescues banks when they run out of money.
Net Interest Margin (NIM)
= Biên lãi suất thuần
Loan rate − Deposit rate = Bank's profit. Main income source.
Collateral
= Tài sản thế chấp
An asset (e.g. house) pledged to the bank to secure a loan.
Fixed Deposit
= Tiết kiệm có kỳ hạn
Money locked for 6–12 months. Higher interest rate than savings account.
Mortgage
= Vay mua nhà
Long-term loan to buy a house. The house is the collateral.
SWIFT Transfer
= Chuyển tiền quốc tế
International bank transfer between countries.
Trade Finance
= Tài trợ thương mại
Banking services that help companies pay for imports and exports.
Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)
= Mua bán & Sáp nhập
When one company buys or merges with another. Investment banks advise on this.
Forex (Foreign Exchange)
= Ngoại hối / Đổi tiền tệ
Converting one currency to another. E.g. VND → EUR.
