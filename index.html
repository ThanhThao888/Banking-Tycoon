VOCAB GUIDE
10 KEY TERMS
Study Before Playing
Central Bank
= Ngân hàng Trung ương
Controls money supply, lends to banks in crisis, issues banknotes.
Lender of Last Resort
= Người cho vay cuối cùng
The Central Bank's emergency role — rescues banks when no one else will lend.
Net Interest Margin (NIM)
= Biên lãi suất thuần
Loan rate − Deposit rate = Bank's profit margin.
Collateral
= Tài sản thế chấp
An asset pledged to secure a loan (e.g. house, gold, vehicle).
Fixed Deposit
= Tiết kiệm có kỳ hạn
Money locked for a set period (e.g. 12 months) for a higher interest rate.
Mortgage
= Vay mua nhà
Long-term loan to buy property. The property is the collateral.
SWIFT Transfer
= Chuyển tiền quốc tế
Global system banks use to send money across countries.
Trade Finance
= Tài trợ thương mại
Banking services that help companies pay for imports and exports.
M&A (Mergers & Acquisitions)
= Mua bán & Sáp nhập
When one company buys or merges with another. Investment banks advise on this.
Forex (Foreign Exchange)
= Ngoại hối
Converting one currency to another. Spot = now. Forward = future rate locked today.
