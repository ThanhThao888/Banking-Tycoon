VOCAB GUIDE
12 KEY TERMS
Study Before Playing
Commercial Bank
= Ngân hàng Thương mại
The bank most people use daily — accepts deposits, gives loans, earns profit from the gap between the two rates.
Central Bank
= Ngân hàng Trung ương
The most powerful bank. Controls money supply, rescues banks in crisis, issues banknotes. E.g. SBV (Vietnam), Fed (USA).
Investment Bank
= Ngân hàng Đầu tư
Does NOT take public deposits. Helps companies raise money via stocks/bonds and advises on M&A. E.g. Goldman Sachs.
Retail Banking
= Ngân hàng Bán lẻ
Banking services for individual customers — savings accounts, personal loans, mortgages, credit cards.
Corporate Banking
= Ngân hàng Doanh nghiệp
Banking for businesses — large loans, trade finance, cash management. Not for individuals.
Lending
= Cho vay
The bank lends depositors' money to borrowers at a higher interest rate. Types: personal loans, mortgages, business loans.
Deposit
= Tiền gửi
Money placed in a bank. Two types: savings account (anytime withdrawal, low rate) and fixed deposit (locked period, higher rate).
Lender of Last Resort
= Người cho vay cuối cùng
The Central Bank's emergency role — rescues banks when no other lender will help.
Net Interest Margin (NIM)
= Biên lãi suất thuần
Loan rate − Deposit rate = Bank's profit margin. E.g. 9% − 4% = 5% NIM.
Collateral
= Tài sản thế chấp
Asset pledged to secure a loan. If you can't repay, the bank takes it. E.g. house for a mortgage.
SWIFT Transfer
= Chuyển tiền quốc tế
The global messaging network banks use to send money across borders.
Forex (Foreign Exchange)
= Ngoại hối
Converting one currency to another. Spot = now. Forward = future date, rate locked today to manage risk.
